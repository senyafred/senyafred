## Hi, I'm Frederick Senya 👋

**PhD Candidate in Mathematical Sciences** @ University of Vermont · **Computational & data modeler** · **Mathematics educator**

I build mathematical models for real-world problems in biology, public health, and clinical data — then make the results clear to whoever needs them. First-author published, with 4+ years teaching university-level mathematics.

📍 San Jose, CA · 📫 senyafrederick@gmail.com · 🔗 [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/frederick-senya)

### 🔬 What I work on

- **Mathematical modeling** — differential equations, reaction-diffusion PDEs, flux-balance analysis, compartmental (disease) models
- **Statistical & data analysis** — Bayesian inference, time-series, regression, predictive modeling, anomaly detection
- **Reproducible scientific computing** — documented, validated, publication-quality workflows

- **First-author publication** — *Equation-Based Integration of Flux Balance Analysis with Diffusion for Spatio-Temporal Simulation of Microbial Communities.* Senya et al., 2026, [bioRxiv](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2026.04.11.717857)
- **COMETS** — recognized contributor to [Computation of Microbial Ecosystems in Time and Space](https://github.com/segrelab/comets) (open-source scientific software), 2025
- **CKD risk modeling** — co-authored predictive model risk-stratifying chronic kidney disease patients by stage degeneration (presented at SIAM)
